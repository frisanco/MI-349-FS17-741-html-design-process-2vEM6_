This exploration, however, is not one that only a single person can accomplish. This will require hardwork, dedication, and most importantly strong leaders and teamwork. Think you've got what it takes to look beyond our skies, to boldly go where no one has gone before? If so, welcome! Adventure is calling, and the sky is never the limit!
